## OUR SOLUTION: SUBSCRIPTION GUARD [1:30-2:45]
### *[Shift to confident, solution-focused tone]*

We built **Subscription Guard** — a platform that puts power back in users' hands.

Here's what we created:

### **1. SUBSCRIPTIONS DASHBOARD**
Every subscription in one place. You can see:
- **What you're paying** – The exact amount and frequency
- **When it renews** – The next charge date highlighted
- **Risk level** – Visual alerts for high-risk subscriptions
- **Cancellation status** – Active, Cancelling, or Cancelled

The goal? Understand your subscription universe in under 5 seconds.

### **2. RENEWAL CALENDAR**
A timeline view of all upcoming charges. Never be surprised by a renewal again. You see exactly when money is leaving your account — days in advance.

### **3. DISPUTE STUDIO – OUR INNOVATION**
This is where we solve the biggest pain point: **fighting back with proof.**

When you get an unexpected charge, our Dispute Studio automatically builds your case:

**Timeline Section:**
- Event 1: Free trial started
- Event 2: Trial was supposed to end April 8
- Event 3: Charge hit April 9 (1 day early)
- Event 4: User attempted cancellation
- Event 5: Cancellation was never processed

**Evidence Section:**
- Transaction details: Exact amount, date, merchant
- Subscription agreement: What you agreed to vs. what happened
- Cancellation records: Proof you tried to stop it
- Communications: All emails and alerts (or missing alerts)
- Confidence score: 87% — tells you how strong your case is

**Export & Action:**
- Download everything as a PDF
- Auto-generate an email to your bank or the merchant
- Submit your proof with one click

This transforms a confusing dispute into a clear, organized, trustworthy case.
