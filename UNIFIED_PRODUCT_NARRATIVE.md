## THE WORLD I WAS BORN INTO

I exist because of a question most people can't answer: **How many subscriptions do you actually have?**

Think about it. Netflix. Spotify. Cloud storage. Adobe Creative Suite. Slack. Gym membership. That productivity app from three months ago. The streaming service you forgot to cancel.

Most people can't name them all. That's not a coincidence. It's intentional design.

I was created because subscriptions are built to be forgotten. They thrive on invisibility. Companies know that if you don't see the charge, you won't cancel. If you don't cancel, they keep getting paid. Week after week. Month after month. Year after year.

Here's what happens most of the time:

**Week 1:** User signs up for Netflix free trial. Excited. Ready to binge.

**Week 2:** They watch a few episodes. Life gets busy.

**Week 3:** A charge appears on their card. $14.99. They forgot the trial ended.

**Week 4:** Another $14.99. And another. Now they're $60 into something they haven't used in weeks.

This story repeats itself across millions of users. Hidden renewals without warning. Forgotten subscriptions people stopped using months ago. Unexpected charges after trials end. Cancellation processes deliberately designed to trap you. Merchants counting on you giving up.

**The result?** The average person loses $200-400 per year to subscriptions they don't use or don't remember. That's $87 billion in annual losses across the United States alone. A billion-dollar problem wearing the mask of a minor inconvenience.

I was created to end this. 

To call it what it is: **Money You Don't Remember Spending.**

## WHO I AM AND WHAT I DO

My purpose is simple: Put power back in users' hands.

I don't judge merchants or condemn subscription businesses. That ecosystem isn't going away. But the game doesn't have to be rigged. Users deserve to see what they're paying for. They deserve to be warned before charges hit. They deserve to fight back when something goes wrong.

I am three things working together:

### **1. SUBSCRIPTIONS DASHBOARD — Your command center**

When users first open me, they see everything at once. No hidden menus. No confusion. Just clarity:

- **What you're paying:** The exact amount and frequency of every subscription
- **When it renews:** Next charge dates highlighted and impossible to miss
- **Risk level:** Visual alerts for high-risk subscriptions flagged in red
- **Cancellation status:** Active, Cancelling, or Cancelled — every state visible

My design principle is simple: **Understand your subscription universe in under 5 seconds.**

Users land on the dashboard. They see they're paying for 12 subscriptions. They thought it was 4. Immediate realization. Immediate action. This is where the power shift begins.

### **2. RENEWAL CALENDAR — Never be surprised again**

I show users a timeline view of all upcoming charges. Every single dollar leaving their account in the next 30, 60, 90 days. Laid out chronologically. Clear as day.

No more surprises. No more "I didn't know that was coming." No more excuses from the merchant saying "We always notify users."

I am the notification. Before the charge. Days in advance. Exactly when users need it.

### **3. DISPUTE STUDIO — My innovation, my pride**

This is where I solve the biggest pain point people face: **Fighting back with proof.**

When users get an unexpected charge, I automatically build their entire case. I don't wait for them to remember emails or search for statements. I compile everything:

**Timeline Section:** Clear, chronological sequence of every event
- Event 1: Free trial started (proven fact)
- Event 2: Trial was supposed to end April 8 (stated agreement)
- Event 3: Charge hit April 9 (the violation - 1 day early)
- Event 4: User attempted cancellation (time-stamped action)
- Event 5: Cancellation was never processed (the injustice)

**Evidence Section:** Organized by category, every piece of proof
- Transaction details: Exact amount, date, merchant, card information
- Subscription agreement: What was promised vs. what actually happened
- Cancellation records: Screenshots and timestamps proving they tried to stop it
- Communications: Every email received (or notably, every email NOT received)
- Confidence score: 87% — this tells users exactly how strong their case is

**Export & Action:** Zero friction to take control
- Download everything as a PDF with one click
- Auto-generate an email to their bank or the merchant
- Pre-filled language that says exactly what happened and why it's wrong
- Send. Done. They've got proof in the system now.

This transforms a confusing, frustrating, impossible-to-win dispute into a clear, organized, trustworthy case. A case that banks can actually act on. A case that gets charges reversed.

## HOW I WORK: TECHNICAL FOUNDATION

I'm not just a nice interface. I'm a complete system, engineered for scale.

### **Backend (The Engine)**

I run on Node.js and Fastify. I handle:

- **Authentication:** Secure login and session management
- **Subscriptions API:** Complete CRUD for every user's subscriptions
- **Dashboard Aggregation:** Real-time calculation of metrics (monthly spend, risk band, next renewal)
- **Timeline Compilation:** Automatic event collection and organization
- **Dispute State Management:** Tracking every stage of a dispute from creation to resolution
- **Security:** Session isolation so each user's data is completely separate

Every request is stateless. This means I can scale to millions of users without breaking. Each user gets their own isolated environment.

### **Frontend (The Experience)**

I live in Next.js and React. I provide:

- **Authentication Page:** Simple PIN entry (locked demo experience, but ready for OAuth in production)
- **Dashboard:** Subscription list with real-time filters and sorting
- **Renewals Calendar:** Visual timeline of upcoming charges
- **Subscription Detail:** Full billing history, cancellation options, risk controls
- **Dispute Studio:** Timeline, evidence repository, export/email functionality

Every page is responsive. Designed mobile-first because most users will access me on their phones.

### **Design System**

I'm built on principles:

- **Color Coding:** Risk levels (Green/Amber/Red), statuses (Blue/Amber/Gray)
- **Information Hierarchy:** Most critical info first, details organized below
- **Accessibility:** WCAG AA compliant, screen reader support, keyboard navigation
- **Responsive Grid:** 1-3 cards per row depending on device, always readable

### **Security & Privacy**

- Zero token exposure to the frontend
- HTTPS encryption on all communication
- Per-session state isolation (one user's data never touches another's)
- PIN-based authentication (demo), ready for enterprise OAuth

I was built right from day one. Production-ready. Scalable. Secure.
